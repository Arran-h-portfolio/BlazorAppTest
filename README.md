<h1>RotaHub (Automated Rota App)</h1>
<h2>Summary</h2>
<p>This project started from a problem I saw in my own life. Working in a business where rotas (or scheduling)
was done the night before it needed to be out, and adjustments were always needed after the fact</p>
<p>The owner who is in charge of scheduling always claimed to be busy or just forgetful. On top of this the rotas came out through Whatsapp as a picture of his laptop screen! I saw a need. 
  An Automated Rota App and a central hub for employers and employees to view and gain a copy of the 
  coming weeks rota at the touch of a button</p>
<h3>How It Works</h3>
<p>You input your staff, their working days/availability, preferred hours, 
  set their status as manager or regular staff all of which can be modified after the fact as staff members or their requirements change
you could then churn out a rota that can be viewed online or printed by the employer as a pdf to be sent out to employees</p>

<h4>The Outcome</h4>
<div>
  <ul>
    <li>Takes the stress off the person creating the rota</li>
    <li>Makes it easier for staff to view the rota</li>
    <li>Much more reliable rotas in a reliable time frame</li>
  </ul>
</div>
